About me

Elizabeth

My full name is Elizabeth Grace Louie. My parents named me that in the hopes that I would be graceful. If it's any indication of my gracefulness, I quit ballet after one year.

I attend Hunter College High School. Most of the time, you'll find me in the back of a classroom with my sketchbook. Yeah, I'm that kid. One of my hobbies is drawing/sketching, specifically drawing people. I also like writing and staring off into space imagining different situations and scenarios. As an only child, these are the ways I've found keep me busy the longest. I'm also a pianist and I've been playing for 8 years now. I'm level 8 ABRSM and level 6 NYSSMA.
